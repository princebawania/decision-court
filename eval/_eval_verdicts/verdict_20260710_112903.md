# Decision Memo: Should I spend six more months hunting for a better AI/ML role, or take the placement offer now?

**Verdict: take the placement offer now**
**Confidence: 80%**

## Why
1. The demand for AI and ML professionals is high, with a 54% increase in demand as reported by the Naukri JobSpeak report
2. Normal online applications for AI and ML jobs can take 8-16 weeks, while passive search can take 4-6 months, making it uncertain to find a better role within six months
3. The placement offer provides financial stability and a head start in career growth, aligning with the decision-maker's priorities

## This verdict flips if
- new evidence emerges that the placement offer has limited growth prospects or low financial rewards
- a better job offer is received within the six-month timeframe with significantly higher financial rewards and growth prospects

---

## The case that was argued

### For: spend six more months hunting for a better AI/ML role
Spending six more months hunting for a better AI/ML role is a strategic decision that aligns with the current market trends and your priorities of career growth, financial stability, and learning curve. The AI job market is highly competitive, with significant investments and growth, as seen in the $45 billion AI investments in 2022 and Canva's AI revenue surge to $2.5B in 2024. This growth indicates a high demand for skilled professionals, and taking the time to find the right role can lead to better opportunities and higher financial rewards.

**Rebuttal from the other side:** My opponent's argument relies on unverified claims, including Canva's alleged $2.5B AI revenue surge and the percentage of the private workforce in AI/ML startups. Without concrete evidence, these assertions are speculative and misleading. Furthermore, their strategy of waiting six months for a better role is risky, as it assumes the market will remain favorable and their skills will remain in demand. This uncertainty undermines their argument, making it a gamble rather than a strategic decision.

### For: take the placement offer now
Given the highly competitive and evolving job market for AI and ML professionals, with a 54% increase in demand as reported by the Naukri JobSpeak report, it's crucial to secure a placement offer now. This demand surge, driven by non-IT sectors, indicates a pivotal shift in the market. Considering the job search timeline, where normal online applications can take 8-16 weeks and passive search can take 4-6 months, taking the placement offer now ensures financial stability and a head start in career growth. Moreover, the demand for skilled ML professionals is soaring, and industries are adopting AI-based solutions rapidly.

**Rebuttal from the other side:** While the demand for AI and ML professionals is indeed high, my opponent's argument relies on a narrow time frame and limited job market perspective. The Naukri JobSpeak report's 54% increase is a snapshot of August 2025, and we don't know if this trend will continue. Moreover, the job search timeline they provided is general and may not apply to our specific situation. With no concrete evidence on the placement offer's quality and growth prospects, it's premature to prioritize financial stability over potential long-term benefits.
